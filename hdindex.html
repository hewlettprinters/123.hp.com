Easy Troubleshooting steps to fix your printer!

Please follow some basic steps to fix your printer problems

For Wi-Fi or Wireless Printer's

Step 1

Press & Hold the printer's power/wi-fi button for 5 seconds and release

Step 2

Go to your device settings and search for your printer

Step 3

In your settings check for printer's drivers status. if required please update or visit our support section

Step 4

Now try to print a test page. It is essential to ensure that your printer's cartridges contain an adequate amount of ink

For Wired or USB Printer's

Step 1

Press & Hold the printer's power button for 5 seconds and release

Step 2

Go to your device settings and check for your printer/plug in your printer USB cable in your device

Step 3

In your settings check for printer's drivers status. If required please update or visit our support section

Step 4

Now try to print a test page. It is essential to ensure that your printer's cartridges contain an adequate amount of ink.
